Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about access, registration, module learning, resources, quizzes, and support channels.

Who is this platform for?

MAM Online Youth Training is specifically for youth responsibles and trainees in the Marial Apostolic Movement who are following the hybrid formation pathway.

How do I register?

Go to the Registration page, create your account, and submit your profile details. Your access stays pending until an admin approves your registration.

How do I access trainings?

Open the Training Catalogue, choose one of the five main modules, then select any training card. Trainings are publicly visible in this stage.

Where can I find resources?

The Resources page groups assets by module and type (PDFs, videos, articles, prayers, workshop files, announcements, and links). Use filter chips for faster navigation.

How do quizzes and completion work now?

Quiz and completion steps are currently UI placeholders. The platform includes clear hooks for FUTURE QUIZ VALIDATION and FUTURE PROGRESS TRACKING when backend services are added.

Who should I contact if I need help?

Use the Contact page for WhatsApp and email placeholders. These can be replaced with official communication channels once approved.
