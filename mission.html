Mission and Vision

Our mission is to equip youth responsibles through integrated spiritual, educational, and pastoral formation. Our vision is a generation of servant leaders rooted in Christ and active in youth mission.

Mission

Form Leaders Who Accompany Youth with Wisdom

We provide structured trainings that strengthen person-centered education, emotional maturity, pastoral leadership, and witness-based service.

رسالتنا إعداد مسؤولين قادرين على مرافقة الشبيبة بروح رسولية ونضج تربوي.

Vision

A Unified Movement of Trained Responsibles

We envision local teams connected by one formation culture, sharing resources, learning standards, and a common mission language.

رؤيتنا حركة شبابية موحدة، تحمل رسالة المسيح بخدمة منظمة وشهادة حيّة.
